3. A은행 청구 업무
   카드 이용 데이터를 기반으로 고객별 청구 금액을 산정하고 청구서를 생성하는 업무

4. B증권 청구 업무

  소득공제 업무가이드.ppt

- 업무설명
  A은행 카드 승인 및 매입 데이터를 기반으로 소득공제 대상 거래를 판별하고 결과를 내부 시스템 및 외부 제출 파일로 생성한다.

- 배치작업
  1단계 (병렬)
    BATCH_01_CARD_APPROVAL_LOAD
    BATCH_02_CARD_PURCHASE_LOAD
    BATCH_03_CUSTOMER_INFO_LOAD
    BATCH_04_MERCHANT_INFO_LOAD
    BATCH_05_CANCEL_DATA_LOAD

  2단계
    BATCH_06_DEDUCTION_TARGET_FILTER
    BATCH_07_DEDUCTION_AGGREGATION

  3단계
    BATCH_08_DEDUCTION_FILE_GENERATION

- 배치흐름도 설명
  병렬:
    BATCH_01_CARD_APPROVAL_LOAD
    BATCH_02_CARD_PURCHASE_LOAD
    BATCH_03_CUSTOMER_INFO_LOAD
    BATCH_04_MERCHANT_INFO_LOAD
    BATCH_05_CANCEL_DATA_LOAD

  순차:
    BATCH_06_DEDUCTION_TARGET_FILTER
    BATCH_07_DEDUCTION_AGGREGATION
    BATCH_08_DEDUCTION_FILE_GENERATION

- 테이블 관계도
  TB_CARD_APPROVAL
  TB_CARD_PURCHASE
  TB_CUSTOMER_INFO
  TB_MERCHANT_INFO
  TB_CANCEL_DATA
  TB_DEDUCTION_TARGET
  TB_DEDUCTION_AGG
  TB_DEDUCTION_RESULT

- 테이블 리니지 설명
  TB_CARD_APPROVAL -> TB_DEDUCTION_TARGET
  TB_CARD_PURCHASE -> TB_DEDUCTION_TARGET
  TB_CUSTOMER_INFO -> TB_DEDUCTION_TARGET
  TB_MERCHANT_INFO -> TB_DEDUCTION_TARGET
  TB_CANCEL_DATA -> TB_DEDUCTION_TARGET

  TB_DEDUCTION_TARGET -> TB_DEDUCTION_AGG
  TB_DEDUCTION_AGG -> TB_DEDUCTION_RESULT
  TB_DEDUCTION_RESULT -> TB_DEDUCTION_FILE
  TB_DEDUCTION_RESULT -> TB_DEDUCTION_LOG
